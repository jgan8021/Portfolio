The PigeonPortfolio of Jingxi Gan

Idea generationBrainstorming 

Our aim is improving borrowing experiences for students by fixing issues they were and currently facing in book’s borrowing process with a use of autonomous device. Students can use this app to borrow books from library. The drone will then deliver orders to user’s location and when the due day come, Pigeon will remind its user and send a drone to pick up and return those books.

"pigeon Concept"STORYBOARDS

The story talks about a student who lives far away from library, and he feels inconvenient to go to library. Then he uses the App "Pigeon" to order books from library. The library staff receives his order and use a drone to delivery the book. Just several minutes, the drone arrives with the book he ordered. A week after, the App reminds the student to return the book and library will send a drone to pick up the order. When the drone arrives, student just need follow the prompt on the App to put the book back to the drone.

Process of High fidelity prototyeRefine

 Through the usability testing, participants recommended me to check for the "return" buttons of all pages to make sure it works properly. The problem regarding misclicks needs to be reconsidered and integrated with some kinds of function to prevent it from happening. Users believed "add to list" and "borrow" buttons should be merged into one or replaced with something more helpful. Participants enjoyed the face recognition system, nevertheless, they expected us to add extra methods of recognition in case that system is malfunction or unreliable. With all those improvements, they positively think this app will be "good to go".

The High fidelity prototyePigeon final iteration

I tested everything all over again . "Add to list" button was replaced with a "list" and "love" icons, hence users believed this is a smart change that proved useful. Besides face id system, at the moment, I integrated an extra manual recognition to cope with the problem they encountered before. All pages in the app was checked carefully to make sure every buttons are going according to plan. Some minor changes were adjusted, such as the change of appearance of "back" icon, the location of notifications, really improved the coherent of the application interface. Participants admitted that this one is a most completed functioning app we have got so far.
